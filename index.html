Periodic Trends
Trend
Atomic Radius
Increases ↓ down a group (more electron shells). Decreases → across a period (more protons pull electrons closer).
Trend
Ionization Energy
Increases → across a period. Decreases ↓ down a group. First IE always lowest; successive IEs get larger.
Trend
Electronegativity
Increases → across a period. Decreases ↓ down a group. F is most electronegative (3.98). Cs is least (0.79).
Trend
Electron Affinity
Generally more negative across a period (want electrons more). Halogens have highest EA. Noble gases near zero.
